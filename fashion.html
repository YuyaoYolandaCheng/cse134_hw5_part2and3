MY FASHION TALK

Milktea Colored Lipstick

Winter is always a time when warm colors are in. The most popular color this winter is definitely milk tea. Many have launched milktea colored lipsticks. Among them, my favorite is Estee Lauder's milk tea lipstick, it is moist and natural.

Peach Perfume

This winter we refuse heavy fragrance! The most popular drink this year is probably white peach oolong tea. Using a peach fragrance in winter is refreshing and sweet.

Cashmere Sweater

The most important characteristic of cashmere sweaters is their softness. They can be worn close to the body. A warm and soft sweater is much needed in winter.

Dior Lipglow

Dior Lip Glow with fresh color and moisturizing cream is a product I use all year round. It has a natural color and lip protection effect. I carry it with me almost every day. I love a high-impact lip color, but in the winter, my thirsty lips aren't always up for supporting a heavy matte lipstick. Pigmented lip balm sounds like a great alternative. The lip glow applied super smoothly and, after a couple coats, gave my lips a pop of color a bit pinker and brighter than natural.
